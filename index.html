Ideation & Research

Our work is novel because it relies on a creative design process, and it brings business value because of our deep industry expertise in manufacturing, marketing, and financial services. A collaborative process to iterate through prototypes allows us to help you find ways to capture business value.

To help Foundation Radiology Group make better strategic decisions, we built a sales and marketing intelligence tool that allowed real-time assessment of radiology related sales triggers.

Design Thinking & Prototyping

We’ve worked with organizations of all sizes to help explore and define the future of business value, so the resulting project plans can be prioritized by executive leadership. This is done through partnering with the business units in a design thinking approach, and building out project plans in the existing organizations project methodology.

Kentucky Eagle Distributing saw an opportunity in better tracking field maintenance work, so we built a tablet based platform that tracked maintenance work and feed that customer data into a sales and marketing platform.

Code Libraries & Front End Frameworks

It can be difficult to find the way from product design to code; we have the experience of defining needs through a design thinking approach, then delivering user interface code libraries to be consumed by development teams. This methodology isolates much of the subjectivity and ambiguity of delivering user value, allowing your back-end developers to focus on delivering business value.

IFC World Bank was developing a new platform to manage risk in portfolios of loans for it’s Global Trade Liquidity Program, and we ideated wireframes with business stakeholders, then built HTML, CSS, and JS to be used in a ReactJS environment.

Oversight & Center of Excellence

Monitoring projects as they progress is essential to make sure the planned value is delivered. We have robust experience in monitoring and correcting for the product related problems that can creep up in projects. Our experience encompasses portfolio project governance and establishing user experience Center of Excellence practices.

As Kroger built a new platform for managing logistics and financing of non-food items we provided project governance and user interface management to ensure a consistent product.
